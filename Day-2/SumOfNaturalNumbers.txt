Sum Of Natural Numbers:
----------------------

Algorthim:
---------

1. Read the numbers.

2. Apply the formula: (n*(n+1))/2.

3. Print the sum.

Approach-2:
----------

1. Read the numbers.

2. Declare the sum variable.

3. Use for loop, repeat until 'n' and increment the sum.

4. Print the sum.

Approach-1 takes O(1) time complexity, whereas Approach-2 takes O(n) time Complexity.
